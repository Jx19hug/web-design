❄️ What’s Santa’s least favorite thing to uncover at a dig site? A cold case!

Day 8

A gentle snowfall blanketed the archaeology dig site, turning the rows of tents and tools into a peaceful winter scene.
Lanterns glowed warmly against the drifting flakes, and strings of simple Christmas lights flickered between the equipment stands, giving the whole area a festive shimmer.
Despite the cold, researchers worked carefully at the edge of a newly uncovered structure, their breath forming small clouds as they brushed away snow to reveal hints of the past.
The mix of holiday cheer and ancient mystery made the snowy site feel almost magical—like history itself was offering a seasonal surprise.
