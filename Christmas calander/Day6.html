Day 6

The Christmas Superhero Headquarters is a festive, high-tech base where heroes gather to plan their missions during the holiday season.
Out with twinkling lights, decorated trees, and stockings hung along sleek control panels, the HQ blends heroics with holiday cheer.
Heroes train, strategize, and even exchange gifts in the cozy lounge, while the workshop hums with gadgets and seasonal surprises.
It's a place where action meets celebration, combining the excitement of saving the day with the warmth and joy of Christmas.
