Children in need

Children in Need is a BBC charity that raises money to support disadvantaged children by funding food, clothing, mental-health services, and safe community projects; this year’s appeal has raised £45,507,536 so far. Pudsey Bear, the charity’s well-known mascot, features in fundraising events across the UK, including in Aberdeen where local groups are taking part in activities such as sponsored walks and community challenges to help boost the total and support children who rely on these vital services.
